Integration is simple and low-cost on Bitcoin:**Setup**: Merchant gets a Bitcoin wallet (e.g., Sparrow or Electrum, free). They run a Bitcoin node or use a service like Blockstream API for on-chain checks (no need for fancy tech).
**Allowance Claim**: Merchant "enters" the system with a 1-sat transaction to a VFT smart address (via Runes protocol). This establishes their account and auto-distributes annual VFT allowance (e.g., equivalent to $1000 in value, but no fiat involved).
**Accepting VFT**: When a customer "pays" for fun (e.g., show tickets, slot spins), they send VFT to the merchant's Bitcoin address. The merchant confirms the transaction on-chain (Bitcoin's 10-min blocks are fine for non-instant use). No fiat conversion — merchant stakes or trades VFT for more allowances or goods within the system.
**No Fiat Cost**: Merchants provide the fun "for free" in fiat terms because they get reimbursed in VFT allowances from the energy-generated pool. It's like bartering with a token backed by energy/production, not dollars.
**Tools**: Use Bitcoin libraries (e.g., python-bitcoinlib) for backend integration. For NFT boosts (Vegas-themed), merchants check user's Ordinals NFTs on-chain to apply extra perks.

Integration takes a developer 1–2 weeks, costs almost nothing (Bitcoin fees ~$1/tx), and aligns with the anti-fiat philosophy — no exchanges, no banks.### How the Average Person Accesses It (in Layman's Terms)
The system is designed for anyone to join without buying tokens or using fiat — just participate, get your allowance, and have fun. Here's how a regular person does it:**Get Started**: Download a Bitcoin wallet app (e.g., BlueWallet or Muun, free on phone). Create an account — takes 2 minutes, no ID needed.
**Enter the System**: Send a tiny "entry" transaction (1 sat, ~$0.00005) to a public VFT address (like a registration smart contract via Runes). This establishes your account and auto-credits your annual allowance (~$1000 equivalent in VFT for Vegas fun). No purchase, no fiat — it's like signing up for a free newsletter but on Bitcoin.
**Get Allowance**: The system (backed by energy-generated VFT pool) airdrops your yearly share automatically. Use it for merchant deals (e.g. free show tickets, casino credits) — scan a QR code or tap "pay with VFT" in the wallet app.
**Stake & Earn**: Stake your VFT in the system (via wallet app or simple web dApp) to earn more (10% APY). If you hold Vegas NFTs (earned or airdropped), get boosts (+20% for 1, +50% for 5+).
**Spend & Trade**: Pay merchants with VFT (instant confirmation, tiny fees). Trade with others peer-to-peer on Bitcoin — no exchanges required.
**Security**: Your wallet is your bank — no KYC, no central authority. Average person accesses everything from their phone, no tech skills needed.
